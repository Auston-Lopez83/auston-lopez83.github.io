Music and Coding

As a musician, I am critical of many things.
I practice to memorize, I read books to learn, and I ask questions to understand.
With programming it is no different.
Music and programing are very similar as they are both filled with many layers that are needed to be complete.

We have the coding language

This is like the bass notes in a piece of music.
They are both the foundation. While today in music we have one standard notation coding has many.

The programmer

The programmer is like the composer. They command the language through careful studying.
Writing, rewriting, being satisfied, then tossing everything out and starting all over.
The process continues till a coherent project is produced.
